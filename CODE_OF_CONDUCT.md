# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We will act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Expected behavior

- Demonstrate empathy and respect for other people, their work, and their boundaries.
- Give and receive constructive feedback in good faith.
- Focus disagreements on ideas, evidence, and project outcomes.
- Protect private information, credentials, and non-public architecture material.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, threats, personal attacks, or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information, credentials, or sensitive project material without permission.
- Sexualized language or imagery, unwelcome attention, or abusive conduct.
- Trolling, insulting comments, or other behavior that a reasonable person would find hostile or exclusionary.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards. They may remove, edit, or reject contributions, comments, commits, code, or other content that does not align with this Code of Conduct.

Report concerns through the repository's private reporting channel when available. If none is available, request a private reporting path without publishing sensitive details. Retaliation against a reporter is not acceptable.
